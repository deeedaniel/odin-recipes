Peanut Sauce

Vietnamese peanut sauce, thick and creamy. Great for spring rolls!

Ingredients:

  • peanut butter
  • chicken broth/pork broth or water
  • ketchup
  • chicken powder
  • sugar & salt
  • a bit of fish sauce
  • Corn starch

Steps:

  1. Combine it all and cook in a pot
  2. Add a little corn starch and water mixture
