McDonald's Fwies :3

McDonald's infamouse fries recipe. Nice and salty

Ingredients:

  • Car
  • Money

Steps:

  1. Drive to McDonalds
  2. Purchase large fries
  3. Ask for ketchup, a few more packets than usual
  4. Get a condiment holder
  5. Drive home
  6. On your computer setup, take out the fries to put in the condiment holder
  7. Open the ketchup packets and fill up the fries box with ketchup
  8. Enjoy :3
