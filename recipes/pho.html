Pho

Pho is a Vietnamese recipe that consists of broth, noodles, meat, rau, and bean sprouts.

Ingredients:

  • Beef bone
  • Filet mignon sliced
  • Beef tendons
  • Rice noodles
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Beef pho powder
  • White onions
  • Green onions
  • Cilantro
  • Basils
  • Bean sprouts

Steps:

  1. Slow cook beef bones with gingers, onions, salt, sugar, seasoning to make beef broth
  2. Low cook beef tendons until soft
  3. Put rice noodles into bowl, put slices of filet mignon and tendons on top
  4. Pour beef broth into same bowl
  5. Put cut up onions, basil, bean sprouts on top
  6. Add any additional sauces you like! Hoison or sriracha.
